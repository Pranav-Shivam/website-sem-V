Department of Biotechnology (UGC Approved & Sponsored)

The UGC approved & sponsored 3 years Hons vocational courses in Biotechnology was started as separate Department in this college from 17th September 2001. The department has already organised many National Seminar and UGC sponsored national Workshops, many pass out student are settled and working in key position in different reputed National & International Pharmaceutical Companies and various Institutions.
